About the Heim Group

With 60 years of experience and over 50,000 Heim/Rousselle units built and used worldwide, The Heim Group takes pride in providing our customers an affordable quality product with the best performance and service. Our single-crank machines range in size from 15 to 300 tons and our straight side presses range in size from 40 to 400 tons. Heim's exclusive Maxi-Stamper Press is available in tonnage capacities from 200 to 1000 tons. Whatever the application, The Heim Group has what it takes to build the press that fits your stamping needs!
